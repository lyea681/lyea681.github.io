About Us

Headquarters founded in Taiwan in 1982. United Integrated Services (USA) Corp is a U.S. subsidiary established in Phoenix in 2020.

United Integrated Services (USA) Corp is a subsidiary of United Integrated Services based in Taiwan. UIS is listed on the stock market and has been partnering with TSMC on projects for decades. UIS continues focusing on innovative, cutting-edge facility technology development and research from design, material, working sequence to construction means and methods as we continue to support TSMC and other foundries with the ever-increasing demand for the chip manufacturing capabilities throughout the world, especially in the United States. United Integrated Services (USA) Corp was established and serves as one of the general contractors on TSMC's Fab 21 in Phoenix, Arizona for our commitment to support customers' global expansion.

"Through innovative design, integrated supply chain and collaborative construction, we provide state-of-the-art design, planning and construction services for high-tech facilities."

CM Lai President-UIS

WHAT WE DO

UIS has been a long-standing MEP and Cleanroom contractor in Taiwan's semiconductor and FPD industry. A multidisciplinary engineering and construction company, UIS partners with owners and subcontractors to manage overall systems coordination. We provide fully integrated design-build services to our clients in the high-technology industry for their manufacturing facility needs. Our scope of services includes engineering design, high-tech factory planning, and on-time equipment and material delivery, in which clean-room systems, HVAC, electrical systems, and tool hook-up, are all vital parts in need of total coordination. Our value-added integrated turnkey services not only provide continuity from planning, design, procurement, construction, tool hook-up, and start-up/commissioning, but also go beyond after service by assisting our clients in facility operation and maintenance. United Integrated Services (USA) Corp currently is managing over 30 subcontractors which represents more than 2,800 employees on site. In the coming months, that number will expand to more than 3,000 workers. The company is also responsible for Air Abatement System (AAS), MEP, and cleanroom.
